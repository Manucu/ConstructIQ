# Project Health

> **Build once.**
>
> **Record once.**
>
> **Trust forever.**

---

# Purpose

Project Health provides a single, understandable indicator of the current condition of a construction project.

Rather than relying on intuition, Project Health evaluates multiple project dimensions and combines them into an overall score.

The objective is to help project stakeholders identify risks early and make informed decisions.

---

# Scope

Project Health evaluates:

- Schedule
- Budget
- Documentation
- Daily Operations
- Approvals
- Extra Works
- Safety
- Overall Project Status

---

# Objectives

Project Health helps users:

- monitor project performance
- identify risks early
- improve decision making
- increase transparency
- support project reporting

---

# Overall Score

Project Health is displayed as:

0 – 100

Higher values indicate healthier projects.

---

# Health Categories

## Schedule Health

Measures:

- completed stages
- delays
- overdue milestones

---

## Budget Health

Measures:

- planned budget
- actual costs
- remaining budget
- approved expenses

---

## Documentation Health

Measures:

- uploaded documents
- required documents
- missing documents

---

## Working Day Health

Measures:

- approved Working Days
- missing daily reports
- late submissions

---

## Approval Health

Measures:

- pending approvals
- rejected items
- overdue approvals

---

## Extra Works Health

Measures:

- approved Extra Works
- pending pricing
- pending client approvals

---

## Safety Health

Future Version

May include:

- incidents
- inspections
- safety observations

---

# Health Calculation

Project Health is calculated automatically using approved project data.

Example

Schedule

25%

Budget

25%

Documentation

15%

Working Days

15%

Approvals

10%

Extra Works

10%

Total

100%

Project Health is recalculated after:

- Working Day Approval
- Invoice Approval
- Stage Completion
- Extra Work Approval
- Budget Update
- Project Completion

# Business Rules

- is calculated automatically
