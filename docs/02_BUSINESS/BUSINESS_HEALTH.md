# ConstructIQ Business Health Engine

## Purpose

Business Health is a composite indicator used to evaluate the overall operational and financial condition of a construction company.

It is calculated based on project performance, budget control, profitability, delays and activity data.

---

## Project Health Score

Each project receives a score from 0 to 100.

Formula:

Project Health =
- Budget Control: 30%
- Progress: 25%
- Schedule Performance: 20%
- Documentation: 10%
- Site Activity: 10%
- Risk Level: 5%

---

## Business Health Score

Business Health is calculated using the average performance of all active projects.

Formula:

Business Health =
- Average Project Health: 40%
- Profitability: 25%
- Budget Control: 20%
- Cash Flow: 10%
- Client Satisfaction: 5%

---

## Example

If a company has:

- Average Project Health: 90
- Profitability: 85
- Budget Control: 80
- Cash Flow: 88
- Client Satisfaction: 95

Then:

Business Health =
90 * 0.40 +
85 * 0.25 +
80 * 0.20 +
88 * 0.10 +
95 * 0.05

Result: 86.8 / 100

---

## Interpretation

Score levels:

- 90 - 100: Excellent
- 75 - 89: Good
- 60 - 74: Warning
- 0 - 59: Critical
