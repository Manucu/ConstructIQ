# Reports

> **Build once.**
>
> **Record once.**
>
> **Trust forever.**

---

# Purpose

The Reports module generates professional construction reports using approved project data.

Reports provide a complete and reliable overview of project execution, financial performance and construction progress.

All reports are generated automatically from information already recorded inside ConstructIQ.

---

# Scope

The Reports module covers:

- Daily Reports
- Weekly Reports
- Monthly Reports
- Stage Reports
- Project Reports
- Financial Reports
- Extra Works Reports
- Client Reports

---

# Objectives

The Reports module aims to:

- eliminate manual report creation
- improve transparency
- support decision making
- provide complete project history
- simplify communication with clients

---

# Report Principles

Reports must:

- use approved information only
- be automatically generated
- remain traceable
- support PDF export

---

# Daily Report

Generated after Working Day approval.

Contains:

- Date
- Weather
- Stage
- Activities
- Workforce
- Materials
- Equipment
- Expenses
- Extra Works
- Photos
- Engineer Notes

---

# Weekly Report

Contains:

- Weekly Progress
- Completed Activities
- Budget Consumption
- Workforce Summary
- Material Consumption
- Equipment Usage
- Photos
- Pending Issues

---

# Monthly Report

Contains:

- Monthly Progress
- Budget Status
- Labor Cost
- Material Cost
- Equipment Cost
- Extra Works
- Timeline Summary
- Project Health

---

# Stage Report

Each Stage has its own report.

Includes:

- Progress
- Budget
- Expenses
- Workforce
- Materials
- Equipment
- Photos
- Documents
- Extra Works
- Completion Status

---

# Project Report

Contains:

## General Information

- Project Name
- Client
- Project Manager
- Site Engineer

## Progress

- Overall Progress
- Current Stage
- Remaining Stages

## Financial Summary

- Contract Value
- Approved Extra Works
- Total Project Value
- Planned Budget
- Actual Cost
- Remaining Budget

## Workforce Summary

- Total Workers
- Total Worked Hours

(No confidential rates)

## Materials

- Materials Used

## Equipment

- Equipment Used

## Extra Works

- Approved Extra Works

## Photos

Photo Summary

## Documents

Project Documentation

---

# Financial Report

Visible only to:

- Owner
- Accountant

Contains:

- Labor Cost
- Material Cost
- Equipment Cost
- Transport Cost
- Other Expenses
- Extra Works
- Profit Estimation

Confidential information is never visible to unauthorized users.

---

# Extra Works Report

Contains:

- Description
- Stage
- Requested By
- Approved By
- Labor
- Materials
- Equipment
- Final Selling Price
- Status

---

# Client Report

Visible to the Client.

Contains:

- Progress
- Photos
- Completed Stages
- Timeline
- Approved Extra Works (optional)
- Documents shared by the company

Does NOT include:

- Worker Rates
- Labor Cost
- Internal Profit
- Confidential Expenses

# Permissions

## Site Engineer

Can:

- View Daily Reports
- Generate Draft Reports

Cannot:

- Generate Financial Reports

---

## Project Manager

Can:

- View
- Generate
- Export

---

## Accountant

Can:

- Generate Financial Reports

---

## Owner

Full access.

---

## Client

Read-only access to shared reports.

---

# Business Rules

Reports:

- use approved data only
- cannot modify project information
- remain permanently available
- are linked to the project history
