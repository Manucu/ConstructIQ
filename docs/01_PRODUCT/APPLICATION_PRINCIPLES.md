# ConstructIQ Application Principles

> **Build once.**
>
> **Record once.**
>
> **Trust forever.**

---

## Our Mission

ConstructIQ helps construction companies manage projects by creating a reliable digital twin of every construction site.

The platform is built around one simple idea:

Information should be entered only once, automatically connected across the entire system, and remain trustworthy for the lifetime of the project.

## Vision

ConstructIQ is not only a construction management application.

It is a Digital Twin platform that mirrors the entire lifecycle of a construction project.

Every project should contain all information required to understand, manage and audit the construction process.

---

# Core Principles

## 1. Single Source of Truth

Every piece of information is entered only once.

The application automatically propagates changes to every related module.

Example:

Expense

↓

Stage Budget

↓

Project Budget

↓

Dashboard

↓

Business Health

↓

Reports

---

## 2. No Data Loss

Important information is never permanently deleted.

Instead, ConstructIQ uses:

- archive
- versioning
- corrections
- history

This guarantees full traceability.

---

## 3. Complete Audit Trail

Every important action must record:

- created by
- created at
- modified by
- modified at
- approved by
- approved at

Every change must remain visible.

---

## 4. Approval Workflow

Important information requires approval.

Example:

Site Engineer

↓

Submit Daily Report

↓

Project Manager

↓

Approve

↓

Dashboard Updated

↓

Reports Generated

---

## 5. Role-Based Permissions

Every user has different permissions.

Owner

- full access

Project Manager

- manages projects

Site Engineer

- daily reports
- workers
- photos
- expenses

Accountant

- invoices
- payments
- budgets

Client

- read only

---

## 6. Every Project is a Digital Twin

Each project represents the real construction site.

It contains:

- stages
- workers
- expenses
- materials
- documents
- photos
- reports
- timeline
- approvals

The application should always reflect reality.

---

## 7. Stage-Centered Architecture

Projects are built from stages.

Each stage contains:

- budget
- expenses
- workers
- materials
- photos
- documents
- diary
- progress

---

## 8. Working Day First

Daily work is recorded through Working Days.

Every Working Day contains:

- weather
- workers
- activities
- expenses
- photos
- notes

Reports are generated automatically.

---

## 9. Automatic Calculations

Users never calculate values manually.

ConstructIQ automatically updates:

- project progress
- budget
- stage progress
- Business Health
- Project Health

---

## 10. Explainable Business Health

Business Health must always be explainable.

Users should always understand why a score changed.

Example:

+2
Project completed on schedule

-3
Budget exceeded

+1
Daily reports submitted

---

## 11. AI as an Assistant

Artificial Intelligence never modifies project data.

AI may:

- summarize
- predict delays
- detect risks
- recommend actions

Final decisions always belong to the user.

---

## 12. Enterprise Ready

ConstructIQ must support:

- one project

and

- hundreds of projects

without changing its architecture.

---

## 13. User Experience First

The application should require as few clicks as possible.

Information should be:

- easy to find
- easy to understand
- easy to update

---

## 14. Professional Reporting

Every important module should generate reports.

Examples:

- Daily Report

- Weekly Report

- Monthly Report

- Budget Report

- Project Report

- Company Report

---

## 15. Security by Design

Financial and operational data must always be protected.

Every modification should be:

- authenticated

- authorized

- logged

- recoverable

---

# Long-Term Goal

ConstructIQ aims to become a complete construction management platform that helps companies:

- manage projects

- reduce delays

- control budgets

- improve communication

- create a complete digital history of every construction project.
