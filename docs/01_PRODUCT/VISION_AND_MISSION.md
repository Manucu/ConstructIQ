# Vision

To become the most trusted digital construction management platform, enabling construction companies to manage every project through accurate, connected and transparent information.

ConstructIQ aims to replace disconnected spreadsheets, paper documents and fragmented communication with one centralized platform that reflects the real state of every construction project.

Every construction project should have a reliable Digital Twin that records its complete history.

---

# Mission

Our mission is to simplify construction project management by providing a modern platform where every project, stage, expense, document, worker and decision is connected.

ConstructIQ helps companies:

- reduce administrative work;
- improve collaboration;
- control budgets;
- increase transparency;
- make better decisions based on real-time information.

# Problems We Solve

Construction companies commonly face the following challenges:

- scattered information
- duplicated data
- inaccurate budget tracking
- paper-based daily reports
- missing project history
- delayed decision making
- poor communication between teams

ConstructIQ centralizes all project information into a single connected platform.

# Core Objectives

ConstructIQ is designed around six major objectives.

## 1. Centralize Information

Every important project record should exist in one place.

Projects

Stages

Working Days

Workers

Expenses

Invoices

Photos

Documents

Reports

---

## 2. Increase Transparency

Every important action should be traceable.

Users should always know:

- who created it;
- when it was created;
- who modified it;
- why it changed;
- who approved it.

---

## 3. Improve Budget Control

Owners should always know:

- planned budget;
- actual costs;
- remaining budget;
- labor costs;
- material costs;
- equipment costs;
- project profitability.

---

## 4. Simplify Daily Site Operations

Site Engineers should spend less time completing paperwork and more time managing construction activities.

Daily reports should be generated through normal daily work instead of being written manually at the end of the day.

---

## 5. Build Reliable Project History

Nothing important should ever disappear.

ConstructIQ keeps a complete history of:

- expenses;
- approvals;
- reports;
- documents;
- photos;
- progress updates;
- budget revisions.

---

## 6. Prepare for Intelligent Decision Making

Future versions of ConstructIQ will include Artificial Intelligence capable of:

- detecting project risks;
- predicting delays;
- identifying budget overruns;
- generating reports;
- suggesting corrective actions.

AI will always assist users without replacing human decisions.
