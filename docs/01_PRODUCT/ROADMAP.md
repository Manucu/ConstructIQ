# 🏗️ ConstructIQ Roadmap

## Vision

ConstructIQ is a modern SaaS platform designed for construction companies to manage projects, budgets, site activity, documents and business performance from a single application.

The goal is to replace spreadsheets and disconnected tools with one intelligent platform that helps construction companies make better decisions.
