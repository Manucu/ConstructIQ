# Business Health

> **Build once.**
>
> **Record once.**
>
> **Trust forever.**

---

# Purpose

Business Health provides a high-level overview of the company's operational and financial performance.

It combines information from all active projects into a single business score that helps the Owner identify opportunities, risks and trends.

Business Health is intended for strategic decision-making.

---

# Scope

Business Health evaluates:

- Financial Performance
- Budget Control
- Project Performance
- Operational Efficiency
- Approval Efficiency
- Portfolio Status

---

# Objectives

The Business Health module aims to:

- monitor overall company performance
- identify business risks early
- support strategic decisions
- improve financial control
- increase operational transparency

---

# Core Principle

Business Health is calculated only from approved information.

Draft, pending or rejected records never influence Business Health.

---

# Overall Score

Business Health is displayed as a score between:

0 – 100

Higher values indicate a healthier business.

---

# Health Categories

## Financial Health

Measures:

- Budget Consumption
- Estimated Profit
- Actual Project Costs
- Outstanding Supplier Payments
- Extra Work Value

---

## Project Health

Aggregates Project Health scores across all active projects.

Measures:

- On-Time Projects
- Delayed Projects
- Completed Projects

---

## Budget Health

Measures:

- Projects Within Budget
- Projects Over Budget
- Cost Variance

---

## Operational Health

Measures:

- Approved Working Days
- Stage Completion
- Missing Reports
- Pending Operational Activities

---

## Approval Health

Measures:

- Pending Approvals
- Rejected Items
- Approval Delays

---

## Portfolio Health

Measures:

- Active Projects
- Completed Projects
- Archived Projects

---

# Health Calculation

Business Health is calculated automatically.

Example weighting:

Financial Health        35%

Project Health          25%

Budget Health           15%

Operational Health      10%

Approval Health         10%

Portfolio Health         5%

Total                  100%

Weights may be configurable in future versions.

# Automatic Updates

Business Health recalculates after:

- Working Day Approval
- Invoice Approval
- Stage Completion
- Project Completion
- Budget Revision
- Extra Work Approval

# Explainable Health

Business Health is transparent.

Example:

Business Health: 94

+18

All active projects within budget

+12

No overdue approvals

+10

High average Project Health

-4

Two projects slightly over budget

-2

One delayed stage

Every score adjustment is explained.
